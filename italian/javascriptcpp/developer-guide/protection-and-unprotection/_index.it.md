## [**Encrypt and decrypt Excel files**](/cells/it/javascript-cpp/encrypt-and-decrypt-excel-files/)

## [**Protect and unprotect workbook**](/cells/it/javascript-cpp/protect-and-unprotect-workbook-structure/)

## [**Protect and unprotect worksheet**](/cells/it/javascript-cpp/protect-and-unprotect-worksheets/)
