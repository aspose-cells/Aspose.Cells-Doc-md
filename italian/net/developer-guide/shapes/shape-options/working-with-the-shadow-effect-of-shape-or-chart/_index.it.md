## **Possibili Scenari di Utilizzo**
Aspose.Cells fornisce la proprietà [Shape.ShadowEffect](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/shape/properties/shadoweffect) insieme alla classe [ShadowEffect](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect) per lavorare con l'effetto ombra della forma o del grafico. La classe [ShadowEffect](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect) contiene le seguenti proprietà che possono essere impostate per ottenere diversi risultati secondo i requisiti dell'applicazione.

- [Angolo](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect/properties/angle)
- [Sfocatura](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect/properties/blur)
- [Colore](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect/properties/color)
- [Distanza](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect/properties/distance)
- [TipoPredefinito](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect/properties/presettype)
- [Dimensione](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect/properties/size)
- [Trasparenza](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect/properties/transparency)
