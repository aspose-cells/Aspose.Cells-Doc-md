## **Possibili scenari di utilizzo**
 Aspose.Cells fornisce il[Forma.Riflessione](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/shape/properties/reflection) proprietà insieme a[Effetto riflessione](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect) classe per lavorare con l'effetto di riflessione della forma o del grafico. Il[Effetto riflessione](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect)class contiene le seguenti proprietà che possono essere impostate per ottenere risultati diversi in base ai requisiti dell'applicazione.

- [Sfocatura](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/properties/blur)
- [Direzione](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/properties/direction)
- [Distanza](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/properties/distance)
- [DissolvenzaDirezione](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/properties/fadedirection)
- [RotWithShape](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/properties/rotwithshape)
- [Dimensione](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/properties/size)
- [Trasparenza](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/properties/transparency)
- [Tipo](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/properties/type)
