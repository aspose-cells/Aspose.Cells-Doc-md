## **Olası Kullanım Senaryoları**
Aspose.Cells for Python via .NET, [Shape.shadow_effect](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/shape/shadow_effect) özelliği ve [ShadowEffect](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect) sınıfını sağlayarak şekil veya grafik gölge efektleriyle çalışmayı destekler. [ShadowEffect](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect) sınıfı, uygulama gereksinimlerine göre farklı sonuçlar elde etmek için ayarlanabilen aşağıdaki özellikleri içerir.

- [açı](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect/angle)
- [bulanıklaştırma](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect/blur)
- [renk](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect/color)
- [mesafe](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect/distance)
- [hazır_tür](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect/preset_type)
- [boyut](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect/size)
- [saydamlık](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect/transparency)
