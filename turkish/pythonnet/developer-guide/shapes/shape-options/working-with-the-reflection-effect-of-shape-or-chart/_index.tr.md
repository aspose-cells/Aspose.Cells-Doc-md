## **Olası Kullanım Senaryoları**
Aspose.Cells for Python via .NET, şekil veya grafiklerin yansıma efektleriyle çalışmak için [Shape.reflection](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/shape/reflection) özelliği ve [ReflectionEffect](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect) sınıfını sağlar. [ReflectionEffect](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect) sınıfı, uygulama gereksinimlerine göre farklı sonuçlar elde etmek için ayarlanabilen aşağıdaki özellikleri içerir.

- [bulanıklaştırma](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/blur)
- [yön](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/direction)
- [mesafe](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/distance)
- [solma_yönü](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/fade_direction)
- [şekille_toplam](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/rot_with_shape)
- [boyut](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/size)
- [saydamlık](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/transparency)
- [tür](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/type)
