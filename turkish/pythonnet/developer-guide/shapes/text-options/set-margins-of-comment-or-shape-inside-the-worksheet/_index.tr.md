## **Olası Kullanım Senaryoları**

Aspose.Cells for Python via .NET, herhangi bir şeklin veya yorumu [**Shape.text_body.text_alignment**](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing.texts/fontsettingcollection/text_alignment) özelliği kullanılarak kenar boşluklarını ayarlamanıza olanak tanır. Bu özellik, çeşitli özelliklere sahip [**ShapeTextAlignment**](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing.texts/shapetextalignment) sınıfı nesnesini döner, örneğin [**top_margin_pt**](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing.texts/shapetextalignment/top_margin_pt), [**left_margin_pt**](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing.texts/shapetextalignment/left_margin_pt), [**bottom_margin_pt**](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing.texts/shapetextalignment/bottom_margin_pt), [**right_margin_pt**](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing.texts/shapetextalignment/right_margin_pt) gibi, bunlar üst, sol, alt ve sağ kenar boşluklarını ayarlamak için kullanılabilir.
