## **Posibles escenarios de uso**
 Aspose.Cells proporciona el[Forma.ShadowEffect](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/shape/properties/shadoweffect) propiedad junto con[efecto de sombra](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect) clase para trabajar con el efecto de sombra de forma o gráfico. los[efecto de sombra](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect)La clase contiene las siguientes propiedades que se pueden configurar para lograr diferentes resultados según los requisitos de la aplicación.

- [Ángulo](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect/properties/angle)
- [Difuminar](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect/properties/blur)
- [Color](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect/properties/color)
- [Distancia](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect/properties/distance)
- [PresetType](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect/properties/presettype)
- [Tamaño](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect/properties/size)
- [Transparencia](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect/properties/transparency)
