{{% alert color="primary" %}} 

This topic demonstrates how to delete rows and columns from a worksheet using the Aspose.Cells.GridWeb API. With the help of this feature, developers can delete rows or columns at runtime.

{{% /alert %}} 
## **Deleting Rows**
To delete a row from your worksheet:

1. Add the Aspose.Cells.GridWeb control to a Web Form.
1. Access the worksheet you are deleting rows from.
1. Delete a row from the worksheet by specifying its row index.

## **Deleting Columns**
To delete a column from your worksheet:

1. Add the Aspose.Cells.GridWeb control to a Web Form.
1. Access the worksheet you want to delete columns from.
1. Delete a column from the worksheet by specifying its column index.
