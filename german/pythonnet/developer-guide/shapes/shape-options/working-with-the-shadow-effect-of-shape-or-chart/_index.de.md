## **Mögliche Verwendungsszenarien**
Aspose.Cells für Python via .NET bietet die Eigenschaft [Shape.shadow_effect](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/shape/shadow_effect) zusammen mit der Klasse [ShadowEffect](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect), um den Schatteneffekt von Formen oder Diagrammen zu bearbeiten. Die Klasse [ShadowEffect](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect) enthält die folgenden Eigenschaften, die eingestellt werden können, um unterschiedliche Ergebnisse je nach Anwendungsanforderungen zu erzielen.

- [Winkel](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect/angle)
- [Unschärfe](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect/blur)
- [Farbe](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect/color)
- [Entfernung](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect/distance)
- [Preset-Typ](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect/preset_type)
- [Größe](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect/size)
- [Transparenz](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect/transparency)
