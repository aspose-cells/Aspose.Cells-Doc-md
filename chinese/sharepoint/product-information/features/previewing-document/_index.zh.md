Aspose.Cells for Share Point支持直接从SharePoint预览文档，以下格式支持预览:

- XLS – Microsoft Excel 97 - 2003
- XLSX – Office Open XML
- XLSM, XLTX, XLTM – Office Open XML
- XML – Microsoft Excel 电子表格ML
