Aspose.Cells提供了为[WorkbookDesigner](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.workbookdesigner)设置自定义数据源的选项。API提供了重载方法 [WorkbookDesigner.SetDataSource](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.workbookdesigner/setdatasource/methods/5) ，它将源的名称作为第一个参数，并将实现 [ICellsDataTable](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells/icellsdatatable) 的类的实例作为第二个参数。
