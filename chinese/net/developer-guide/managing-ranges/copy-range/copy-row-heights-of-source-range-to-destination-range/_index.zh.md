{{% alert color="primary" %}}

有时用户需要将源范围的行高复制到目标范围。Aspose.Cells为此提供了[**PasteType.RowHeights**](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells/pastetype)枚举。当您将[**PasteOptions.PasteType**](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells/pasteoptions/properties/pastetype)属性设置为[**PasteType.RowHeights**](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells/pastetype)枚举时，源范围内所有行的高度将被复制到目标范围。

{{% /alert %}}
