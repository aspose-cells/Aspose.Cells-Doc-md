## **可能的使用场景**
Aspose.Cells提供了[Shape.Reflection](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/shape/properties/reflection)属性，以及[ReflectionEffect](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect)类来处理形状或图表的倒影效果。 [ReflectionEffect](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect)类包含以下属性，可以根据应用程序需求进行设置以实现不同的效果。

- [模糊](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/properties/blur)
- [方向](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/properties/direction)
- [距离](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/properties/distance)
- [淡化方向](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/properties/fadedirection)
- [与形状一起旋转](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/properties/rotwithshape)
- [大小](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/properties/size)
- [透明度](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/properties/transparency)
- [类型](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/properties/type)
