## **可能的使用场景**
Aspose.Cells 提供了[形状.反射](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/shape/properties/reflection)财产连同[反射效果](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect)使用形状或图表的反射效果的类。这[反射效果](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect)类包含以下属性，可以根据应用程序要求设置这些属性以获得不同的结果。

- [模糊](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/properties/blur)
- [方向](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/properties/direction)
- [距离](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/properties/distance)
- [渐变方向](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/properties/fadedirection)
- [随形旋转](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/properties/rotwithshape)
- [尺寸](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/properties/size)
- [透明度](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/properties/transparency)
- [类型](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/properties/type)
