## **可能的使用场景**
Aspose.Cells 提供[Shape.Glow](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/shape/properties/glow) 属性以及[GlowEffect](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/gloweffect) 类来处理形状或图表的发光效果。 [GlowEffect](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/gloweffect) 类包含以下属性，可根据应用程序要求设置以实现不同的结果。

- [GlowEffect.Size](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/gloweffect/properties/size)
- [GlowEffect.Transparency](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/gloweffect/properties/transparency)
- [发光效果.颜色](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/gloweffect/properties/color)
