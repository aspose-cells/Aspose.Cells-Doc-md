## **可能的使用场景**
Aspose.Cells 提供了[形状.发光](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/shape/properties/glow)财产连同[发光效果](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/gloweffect)使用形状或图表的发光效果的类。这[发光效果](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/gloweffect)类包含以下属性，可以根据应用程序要求设置这些属性以获得不同的结果。

- [发光效果.大小](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/gloweffect/properties/size)
- [发光效果.透明度](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/gloweffect/properties/transparency)
- [发光效果.颜色](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/gloweffect/properties/color)
