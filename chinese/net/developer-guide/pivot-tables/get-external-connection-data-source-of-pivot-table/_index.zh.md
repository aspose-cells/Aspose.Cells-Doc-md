## **获取数据透视表的外部连接数据源**

Aspose.Cells提供获取数据透视表外部连接数据源的能力。为此，API提供了[**ExternalConnectionDataSource**](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.pivot/pivottable/properties/externalconnectiondatasource)类的[**PivotTable**](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.pivot/pivottable)属性。[**ExternalConnectionDataSource**](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.pivot/pivottable/properties/externalconnectiondatasource)属性返回[**ExternalConnection**](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.externalconnections/externalconnection)对象。以下代码片段演示如何使用[**PivotTable.ExternalConnectionDataSource**](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.pivot/pivottable/properties/externalconnectiondatasource)属性获取数据透视表的外部连接数据源。
