## **查找或搜索数据**

- [查找号码](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/cpp/class/aspose.cells.i_cell#a7f90de51db6984956b5ed0b33b73a111)
- [查找公式](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/cpp/class/aspose.cells.i_cell#ae3a8a9cfe7f8dc84ea590a8472e194f1)
- [查找公式包含](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/cpp/class/aspose.cells.i_cell#ae8d2b8cdef52ef4e501502c4b905943f)
- [查找字符串](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/cpp/class/aspose.cells.i_cell#ac3e27028cf708eba8efb0997f9c9048e)
- [查找字符串包含](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/cpp/class/aspose.cells.i_cell#a4f863b51674f7b18cf20a344872ad704)
- [查找字符串开头为](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/cpp/class/aspose.cells.i_cell#afa17f3d771e73731dd9f682f4ac359df)
