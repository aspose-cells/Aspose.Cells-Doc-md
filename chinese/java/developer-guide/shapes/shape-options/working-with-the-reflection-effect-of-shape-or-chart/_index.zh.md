## **可能的使用场景**
Aspose.Cells 提供了[形状.反射效果](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/java/com.aspose.cells/shape#Reflection)与形状或图表的反射效果一起使用的属性。它包含以下子属性，您可以根据需要设置这些子属性以获得不同的结果。

- [反射效果.模糊](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/java/com.aspose.cells/reflectioneffect#Blur)
- [反射效果.方向](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/java/com.aspose.cells/reflectioneffect#Direction)
- [反射效果.距离](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/java/com.aspose.cells/reflectioneffect#Distance)
- [反射效果.FadeDirection](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/java/com.aspose.cells/reflectioneffect#FadeDirection)
- [反射效果.RotWithShape](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/java/com.aspose.cells/reflectioneffect#RotWithShape)
- [反射效果.大小](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/java/com.aspose.cells/reflectioneffect#Size)
- [反射效果.透明度](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/java/com.aspose.cells/reflectioneffect#Transparency)
- [反射效果类型](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/java/com.aspose.cells/reflectioneffect#Type)
