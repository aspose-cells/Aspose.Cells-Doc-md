## **获取范围内的超链接**

[**Range**](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells/range) 类提供一个 [**hyperlinks**](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells/range/hyperlinks/) 属性，该属性返回所选范围内的所有超链接。您还可以调用 [**Hyperlink.delete**](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells/hyperlink/delete/#) 方法来删除超链接。
