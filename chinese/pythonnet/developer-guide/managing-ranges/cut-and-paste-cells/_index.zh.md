## **剪切和粘贴单元格**

Aspose.Cells提供了通过[**insert_cut_cells **](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells/cells/insert_cut_cells/)集合的[**Cells**](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells/cells)方法在工作表内部剪切和粘贴单元格的功能。[**insert_cut_cells **](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells/cells/insert_cut_cells/)接受以下参数。

- [**Range**](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells/range)：要剪切的单元格范围。
- 行索引：要插入单元格的行索引。
- 列索引：要插入单元格的列索引。
- [**ShiftType**](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells/shifttype)：列的位移方向。
