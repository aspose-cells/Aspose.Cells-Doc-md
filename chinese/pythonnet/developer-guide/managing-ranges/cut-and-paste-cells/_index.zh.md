## **剪切和粘贴单元格**

Aspose.Cells 提供了在工作表中通过使用 [**insert_cut_cells **](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells/cells/insert_cut_cells/) 集合的 [**Cells**](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells/cells) 方法剪切和粘贴单元格的功能。[**insert_cut_cells **](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells/cells/insert_cut_cells/) 接受以下参数。

- [**Range**](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells/range)：要剪切的单元格范围。
- 行索引：要插入单元格的行索引。
- 列索引：要插入单元格的列索引。
- [**ShiftType**](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells/shifttype)：列的移动方向。
