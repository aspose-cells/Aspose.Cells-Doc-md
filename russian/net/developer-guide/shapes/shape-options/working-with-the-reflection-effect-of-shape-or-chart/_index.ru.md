## **Возможные сценарии использования**
 Aspose.Cells обеспечивает[Форма.Отражение](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/shape/properties/reflection) имущество вместе с[ОтражениеЭффект](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect) класс для работы с эффектом отражения фигуры или диаграммы.[ОтражениеЭффект](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect)Класс содержит следующие свойства, которые можно настроить для достижения различных результатов в соответствии с требованиями приложения.

- [Размытие](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/properties/blur)
- [Направление](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/properties/direction)
- [Расстояние](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/properties/distance)
- [FadeDirection](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/properties/fadedirection)
- [RotWithShape](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/properties/rotwithshape)
- [Размер](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/properties/size)
- [Прозрачность](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/properties/transparency)
- [Тип](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/properties/type)
