## **可能な使用シナリオ**
Aspose.Cells for Python via .NETは、[Shape.shadow_effect](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/shape/shadow_effect)プロパティと[ShadowEffect](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect)クラスを提供し、シェイプやチャートの影の効果を操作します。 [ShadowEffect](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect)クラスには、さまざまな結果を達成するために設定可能な以下のプロパティがあります。

- [角度](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect/angle)
- [ぼかし](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect/blur)
- [色](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect/color)
- [距離](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect/distance)
- [プリセットタイプ](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect/preset_type)
- [サイズ](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect/size)
- [透過性](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/shadoweffect/transparency)
