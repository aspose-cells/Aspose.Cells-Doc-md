## **可能な使用シナリオ**
Aspose.Cells for Python via .NETは、[Shape.reflection](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/shape/reflection)プロパティと[ReflectionEffect](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect)クラスを提供して、シェイプやチャートの反射効果を操作します。[ReflectionEffect](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect)クラスには、さまざまな結果を得るために設定可能な以下のプロパティがあります。

- [ぼかし](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/blur)
- [方向](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/direction)
- [距離](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/distance)
- [フェード方向](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/fade_direction)
- [tot_with_shape](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/rot_with_shape)
- [サイズ](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/size)
- [透過性](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/transparency)
- [タイプ](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/type)
