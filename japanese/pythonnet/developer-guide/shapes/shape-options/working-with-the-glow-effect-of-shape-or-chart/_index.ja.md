## **可能な使用シナリオ**
Aspose.Cells for Python via .NETは、[Shape.glow](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/shape/glow)プロパティと[GlowEffect](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/gloweffect)クラスを提供して、シェイプやチャートの発光効果を操作します。[GlowEffect](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/gloweffect)クラスには、さまざまな結果を得るために設定可能な以下のプロパティがあります。

- [GlowEffect.size](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/gloweffect/size)
- [GlowEffect.transparency](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/gloweffect/transparency)
- [GlowEffect.color](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells.drawing/gloweffect/color)
