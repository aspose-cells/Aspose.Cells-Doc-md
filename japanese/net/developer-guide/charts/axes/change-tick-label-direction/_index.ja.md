##  **目盛りラベルの方向を変更する**

Aspose.Cells では、[**TickLabels.DirectionType**](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.charts/ticklabels/properties/directiontype)財産。の[**TickLabels.DirectionType**](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.charts/ticklabels/properties/directiontype)プロパティは、[**ChartTextDirectionType**](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.charts/charttextdirectiontype)列挙値。の[**ChartTextDirectionType**](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.charts/charttextdirectiontype)列挙では次のメンバーが提供されます

- 水平
- 垂直
- 90回転
- 回転270
- 積み重ねられた
