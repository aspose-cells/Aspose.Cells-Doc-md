## **可能な使用シナリオ**
Aspose.Cellsは、形状やチャートのリフレクションエフェクトを操作するための [Shape.Reflection](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/shape/properties/reflection) プロパティと [ReflectionEffect](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect) クラスを提供しています。 [ReflectionEffect](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect) クラスには、アプリケーションの要件に応じて異なる結果を得るために設定できる以下のプロパティが含まれています。

- [ぼかし](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/properties/blur)
- [方向](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/properties/direction)
- [距離](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/properties/distance)
- [フェード方向](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/properties/fadedirection)
- [形状に合わせる](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/properties/rotwithshape)
- [サイズ](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/properties/size)
- [透明性](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/properties/transparency)
- [タイプ](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.drawing/reflectioneffect/properties/type)
