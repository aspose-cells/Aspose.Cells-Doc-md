## **Räkna antalet celler i kalkylbladet**

För detta tillhandahåller API:et [Cells.getCount](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python/asposecells.api/cells#Count)() eller [Cells.getCountLarge](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python/asposecells.api/cells#CountLarge)() egenskaperna.
