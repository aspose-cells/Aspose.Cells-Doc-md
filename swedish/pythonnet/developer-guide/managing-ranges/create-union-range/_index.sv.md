## **Skapa unionsspann**
Aspose.Cells ger möjlighet att skapa Union Range genom att använda metoden [**WorksheetCollection.create_union_range**](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells/worksheetcollection/create_union_range/#str-int). Metoden [**WorksheetCollection.create_union_range**](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells/worksheetcollection/create_union_range/#str-int) accepterar två parametrar, adressen för att skapa Union Range och indexet för kalkylbladet. Metoden [**WorksheetCollection.create_union_range**](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells/worksheetcollection/create_union_range/#str-int) returnerar en [**UnionRange**](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python-net/aspose.cells/unionrange)-objekt.
