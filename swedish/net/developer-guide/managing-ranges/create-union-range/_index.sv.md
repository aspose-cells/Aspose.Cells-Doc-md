## **Skapa unionsspann**
Aspose.Cells ger möjlighet att skapa en Union Range genom att använda metoden [WorksheetCollection.CreateUnionRange](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells/worksheetcollection/methods/createunionrange). Metoden [WorksheetCollection.CreateUnionRange](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells/worksheetcollection/methods/createunionrange) accepterar två parametrar, adressen för att skapa unionrådet och arbetsbladets index. Metoden [WorksheetCollection.CreateUnionRange](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells/worksheetcollection/methods/createunionrange) returnerar ett objekt [UnionRange](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells/unionrange).
