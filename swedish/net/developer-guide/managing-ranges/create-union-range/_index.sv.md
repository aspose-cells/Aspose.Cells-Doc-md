## **Skapa Union Range**
 Aspose.Cells ger möjligheten att skapa Union Range genom att använda[WorksheetCollection.CreateUnionRange](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells/worksheetcollection/methods/createunionrange)metod. De[WorksheetCollection.CreateUnionRange](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells/worksheetcollection/methods/createunionrange)Metoden accepterar två parametrar, adressen för att skapa unionsintervallet och indexet för kalkylbladet. De[WorksheetCollection.CreateUnionRange](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells/worksheetcollection/methods/createunionrange) metod returnerar en[UnionRange](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells/unionrange) objekt.
