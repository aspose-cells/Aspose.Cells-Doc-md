{{% alert color="primary" %}} 

To install Aspose.Cells for JasperReports on your system, your system needs to meet the following requirements:

- Java 2 Platform, Standard Edition (J2SE) 1.6 or higher.
- JasperReports 6.3.0 or higher.

{{% /alert %}}
