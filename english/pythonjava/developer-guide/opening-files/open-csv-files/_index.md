## **Open CSV Files**  
Comma-separated Values (CSV) files contain records whose values are delimited or separated by commas. You may use Microsoft Excel to save your spreadsheet in CSV format.  

To open CSV files, use the [LoadOptions](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python/asposecells.api/LoadOptions) class, and select the [CSV](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python/asposecells.api/fileformattype#CSV) value, predefined in the [FileFormatType](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/python/asposecells.api/FileFormatType) enumeration.
