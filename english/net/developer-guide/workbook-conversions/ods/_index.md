## **About OpenDocument**
The [OpenDocument format (ODF)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/OpenDocument) is a free and open file format for electronic office documents originally developed by Sun for the OpenOffice suite. OpenDocument Spreadsheet (ODS) is the file format for spreadsheet documents, comparable to Excel. OpenDocument is currently an OASIS and ISO standard.
